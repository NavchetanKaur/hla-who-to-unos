HAAMU: HLA allele to antigen mapping for UNOS

Overview

HAAMU web tool facilitates the mapping of molecular HLA nomenclature to serological nomenclature required for entering transplant receipients' unacceptable antigens into UNet system. The tool obliges the guidelines laid by Organ procurement and transplant network for antigen mapping. The tool supports antigen mapping for alleles from six loci i.e. A, C, B, DRB1, DQB1, DR345.
